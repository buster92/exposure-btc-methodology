# FAQ

## Is Exposure BTC a price prediction model?

No. It is an exposure and risk framework.

## Is this financial advice?

No. The project is designed as a quantitative decision-support system focused on regime and risk.

## Why not just buy and hold?

Because market conditions are not equal. Exposure BTC is built on the idea that risk can rise meaningfully even while price continues upward.

## Why not use RSI alone or simple signals?

Single indicators are often too reactive or too narrow. The methodology is built around the interaction between structure, volatility, and drawdown pressure.

## Does this repository reveal the full model?

No. It explains the methodology and public framing, not the private decision engine.

## What makes the framework different?

The core distinction is that it focuses on **exposure quality**, not just direction. It tries to detect when the market is becoming worse for disciplined exposure even before obvious price damage appears.
