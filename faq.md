# FAQ

## Is Exposure BTC a price prediction model?

No. It is an exposure and risk framework.

## Is this financial advice?

No. The project is designed as a quantitative decision-support system focused on market conditions and risk.

## Why not just buy and hold?

Because market conditions are not constant. Exposure BTC is based on the idea that risk can increase even while price continues upward.

## Why not use simple indicators?

Single indicators often fail to capture the full context of market behavior. This framework evaluates multiple dimensions together rather than relying on isolated signals.

## Does this repository reveal the full model?

No. It explains the conceptual approach and public framing, not the internal implementation.

## What makes the framework different?

The core distinction is that it evaluates **exposure quality**, not just price direction. It focuses on whether conditions support disciplined participation rather than assuming all upward movement is equally favorable.
